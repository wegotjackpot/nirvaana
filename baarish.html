That evening,
so unplanned,
but the rain had plans.
And somewhere between
drenched clothes and quiet smiles,
we became something more.
Not defined,
just real.

That night,
we didn’t just get closer —
we got written into each other.
In every drop,
every laugh,
we found a piece of us
we hadn’t known was missing.

And then there was that kiss,
the world pausing,
just for us,
as if the rain itself was holding us
in that moment.
